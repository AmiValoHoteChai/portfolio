Overview

Healthcare Management System is a comprehensive terminal-based application developed in C, designed to streamline hospital operations with a clean and intuitive CLI interface.

The system prioritizes security and organization through role-based access control, allowing different levels of management for Admins, Doctors, and Receptionists.
