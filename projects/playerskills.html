Overview

Player Skills Reborn Again is a powerful Spigot/Paper server plugin designed to enhance the gameplay experience by adding a custom skill system. Players can earn experience in various categories, unlocking unique abilities and bonuses as they level up.

The Inspiration: This plugin isn't just a new idea—it's a revitalization. I used to play a Minecraft plugin years ago that featured a similar system, but the original developer stopped supporting it and it was eventually lost to time. I took that inspiration and rebuilt it from the ground up for modern Minecraft versions, ensuring a legacy of progression continues.
